About GraffNet

Built by street art lovers, GraffNet connects artists, crews, and curious explorers in a shared augmented reality layer. Use the mobile app to discover nearby tags, drop your own streak, and see neighborhoods transform into living galleries.

Location-aware

Lock in precise coordinates or let GPS place your mark instantly.

Community powered

Upvotes bubble the freshest walls to the top while keeping spam in check.

Cross-platform

Designed for iOS, iPadOS, and visionOS with the same Firebase backbone.

Graffiti-native features

GraffNet packs a muralist's studio into your pocket. Here's what the crew has been building:

Tag Map

Browse a live map of nearby spots, zoom in on clusters, and teleport straight to hype murals. Long press to drop a new piece at the map center.

AR Graffiti Canvas

When hardware allows, summon the AR painter and sketch directly onto the world. VisionPro and LiDAR devices deliver ultra-precise overlays.

Realtime Sessions

Flip on live updates to stream new tags the instant they hit. Firebase listeners keep crews synchronized without lifting a finger.

Smart Moderation

Voting controls keep the best art front and center. Downvote to fade weak tags; celebrate heat with quick upvotes.

Offline-first tools

Draft your text tags anywhere. GraffNet queues them up and ships them when you're back on the grid.

Creator analytics

Track upvotes, hotspots, and returning fans with slick dashboards once you connect to the Firebase console.

Experience the spray

From the first ping of GPS to the last drop of digital paint, GraffNet is crafted to feel like bombing a fresh wall without the drama. The UI pulses with neon gradients, while accessibility-tested controls make the app friendly for every crew member.

1. Drop a tag

Use precise coordinates or a long press on the map to mark your turf.

2. Add color

Toggle the AR canvas for freehand spray strokes, bold fills, or sticker imports.

3. Hype it up

Share your GraffNet invite, collect upvotes, and climb the city's leaderboards.

Deploy on GitHub Pages

Bring this site to life right from your repository.

  1. Commit the website folder to your main branch.
  2. In your GitHub repo, open Settings → Pages, choose Main and /website for the folder.
  3. Save, wait for the build, and share your new graffiti hub with the crew.

Want custom domains or advanced routing? Drop a 404.html or configure a CNAME file in this directory before deploying.
